## About Loom

**Loom** is a leading **async video messaging platform** that lets you record your screen, voice, and webcam — then share the video instantly via a link. Founded in 2015 and acquired by **Atlassian in 2023**, Loom is built for distributed teams who want to replace unnecessary meetings with quick, asynchronous video updates.

### What Makes Loom Special

Loom turns the way teams communicate upside down. Instead of writing long emails, scheduling another Zoom meeting, or trying to explain something complex over Slack, you press a button and **show** instead of tell. Within seconds, your colleagues anywhere in the world can watch your video — pause, rewind, react with emoji, leave time-stamped comments, or even reply with their own video.

The platform combines **HD screen recording**, **AI-generated transcripts and summaries**, **engagement analytics**, and **deep integrations** with the tools teams already use — Slack, Notion, Jira, GitHub, Salesforce, and dozens more.

### By the Numbers

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Active Users | 25M+ |
| Companies Using Loom | 400,000+ |
| Languages Supported | 50+ (transcription) |
| Native Integrations | 50+ |
| Founded | 2015 |
| Parent Company | Atlassian (since 2023) |

### Core Philosophy: Async-First

Loom is built around one core idea: **synchronous meetings shouldn't be the default**. By replacing live calls with short, scannable videos, teams reclaim hours every week, accommodate global time zones, and create a searchable archive of decisions, walkthroughs, and updates.

> 💬 *"Instead of typing out a long Slack message, just send a Loom."*
> — A common phrase you'll hear in Loom-powered teams

## Features

A comprehensive overview of what makes Loom one of the most popular async video messaging platforms in 2026.

### Core Recording

- **Screen Recording** — Capture your full desktop, a single application, or a browser tab in HD or 4K
- **Webcam Overlay** — Customizable camera bubble (round, square, or full-screen)
- **Audio Capture** — System audio, microphone, or both with crystal-clear quality
- **Drawing Tools** — Annotate in real-time with arrows, highlights, and pen strokes
- **Click & Cursor Effects** — Visual emphasis to guide viewer attention
- **Pause & Resume** — Stop and continue without restarting
- **Custom Recording Area** — Pixel-precise screen region selection

### AI-Powered Tools

- **Auto-Generated Titles** — AI suggests relevant titles based on content
- **Smart Summaries** — Automatic chapter breakdowns and key takeaways
- **Multi-Language Transcripts** — Auto-transcription in 50+ languages
- **Filler Word Removal** — Automatically removes "ums," "ahs," and pauses
- **Silence Trimming** — Cuts dead air for tighter, engaging videos
- **Auto-Chapters** — AI splits long videos into navigable sections
- **Smart CTAs** — Context-aware call-to-action suggestions

### Editing & Customization

- **Trim & Cut** — Remove unwanted sections without re-recording
- **Stitch Videos** — Combine multiple recordings into one
- **Custom Thumbnails** — Upload or auto-generate eye-catching thumbnails
- **Background Replacement** — Blur or replace your camera background
- **Noise Suppression** — AI-powered background noise removal
- **Custom Watermarks** — Brand videos with logo overlays
- **Branded Workspaces** — Custom colors, logos, and player themes

### Engagement & Interaction

- **Time-Stamped Comments** — Discussions tied to exact video moments
- **Emoji Reactions** — Quick feedback without typing
- **Video Replies** — Respond with another video
- **Viewer Insights** — Detailed analytics on who watched and when
- **Interactive CTAs** — Clickable buttons inside videos
- **Smart Notifications** — Alerts when key viewers watch

### Sharing & Distribution

- **Instant Shareable Links** — Auto-copied to clipboard after recording
- **Embed Anywhere** — Native embeds for Notion, Confluence, Google Docs
- **Password Protection** — Secure sensitive videos
- **Link Expiration** — Time-limited access

### Team Collaboration

## Getting Started

### Step 1 — Sign Up
Head over to [loom.com/signup](https://www.loom.com/signup) and create a free account using your email or Google/Apple SSO.

### Step 2 — Choose Your Recording Method

| Platform | Best For | Where to Get It |
|----------|----------|-----------------|
| **Chrome Extension** | Quick browser captures | [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore) |
| **Desktop App (Mac/Windows)** | Full-featured recording | [loom.com/download](https://www.loom.com/download) |
| **Mobile App (iOS/Android)** | On-the-go video messages | App Store / Google Play |

### Step 3 — Record Your First Video
1. Click the Loom icon
2. Choose **Screen + Cam**, **Screen Only**, or **Cam Only**
3. Hit **Start Recording**
4. Pause, draw, or use the teleprompter as needed
5. Stop and share — the link is automatically copied

## Tips & Best Practices

1. **Plan before recording** — Jot 3 bullet points to keep videos under 5 minutes
2. **Use a good mic** — Audio quality matters more than video quality
3. **Light your face** — Window light beats fancy gear
4. **Trim aggressively** — Cut anything that doesn't add value
5. **Add a clear title and CTA** — Tell viewers exactly what to do next
6. **Use chapters** — Break long videos into navigable sections
7. **Check analytics** — See where viewers drop off and improve
